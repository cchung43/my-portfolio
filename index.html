About Me.

Claire Chung (鍾知辰)

sometimes i try to see as far as i can into the sky, and for a moment, let myself be struck by all of it. i am finally old enough to experience feelings so wide and nameless that they engulf me. and because of that, i wanted to find a space where I could authentically showcase my love for experiencing life, through the hush of an image or the weight of a sentence. the days are so short, but anything can be beautiful.

this portfolio is a collection of: photos i have taken, mini blog posts, concert performances, and anything else i eventually might want to share.

thank you for taking the time to see the world through my eyes.
